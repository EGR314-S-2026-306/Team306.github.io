## Project Objective Overview

The objective of this project is to create subterrianian rover with the ability to drill into dirt and collect samples of different soils and enviroments. The rover will have resistive sensors to measure chemical compositions in the soil determining what to collect and what to ignore. Our Rover Will also be equipped with human interface, giving the operator the ability to set different conditions and routes for the rover to operate. The rover will also include wireless communication recieving information from the user without wired connections.
